The Blue Marlin Hotel.
Party Friendly Boutique Hotel in Jaco Center

Location:

Jaco Beach, Costa Rica. This is the Best location in the center of town. Only a few steps to the Cocal Casino and Pool Bar, Jaco Beach, Famous Nightlife with Discos, Clubs, Bars, and Restaurants, as well as Shopping, Groceries, Liquor Store, Pharmacies, and more.

Accommodations:

10 completely updated hotel rooms with A/C, safe box, TV and new en-suite bathroom. Max occupancy is 2 people per room.

Fully Renovated.

This hotel was given a complete make over with brand-new bathrooms, new tiles throughout, new beds, new mattresses, new furniture, new lighting, new TVs, new sheets and new towels.

Adults Only.

This is a great party friendly hotel and welcomes all groups including those coming to Costa Rica for a bachelor party or bachelorette party. Everything you could want during a vacation in Jaco is within walking distance.

Please Note: Although there are noise restrictions in Jaco, this hotel has a night club on site and is in the center of town surrounded by nightlife and can get noisy sometimes. Also, this property is attached to a private 10 bedroom party friendly vacation rental (The Blue Marlin Party Complex) which shares a common wall and an overnight doorman. The two properties are completely separate and private, but be aware that poolside sound/music/noise does travel across.
