Daytime Activities, Tours & Excursions to do during your Bachelor Party vacation in Jaco!

From ATV to Zip line.

Sure, there will likely be plenty of debauchery in the evenings and nights, and some morning hangovers could play out, but you are in Costa Rica and MUST take advantage of the awesome daytime tours, activities, excursions and many things to do available in the Jaco Beach area.

Jaco VIP offers the most exhilarating, professional and safest tours and excursions in Jaco, and all at great prices! We have carefully chosen local tour operators who can offer our guests amazing and memorable experiences.

The 4 Hour ATV Tour to a Waterfall has become a bachelor party standard in our neck of the woods. With or without ATV experience, you will have a blast riding on dirt road trails, in fresh water rivers, and through small rural towns.

A recent addition to the motorized tours in Jaco are Mini Dirt Bikes which are offered on a private track where you can race with your friends, or can be used on similar excursions as the 4 wheel ATVs. At 125 cc and approxiamtely half the size of traditional dirt bikes, these are much easier to manouver and just as much fun.

The local zip line canopy tour is world class. A Costa Rica must do excursion with a dozen cables nestled within a jungle and rainforest mountainside. The waterfall rappelling and canyoning tour has also become very popular with our bachelor party groups in the last few years.

Jaco Beach is one of the worlds best places to learn to surf. The south end is ideal for beginners with warm shallow water and lessons with experienced instructors. For more advanced surfers, 15 minutes to the South is Playa Hermosa which has hosted many surf competitions as well as famous and pro surfers.

For the golf enthusiasts, La Iguana Golf Course is located 20 minutes away at Los SueÃ±os Resort. An 18 hole course designed by Ted Robinson in the heart of lush tropical surroundings. PGA certified golf instructors available.

If you're looking to see more of the country, a day trip excursion to Manuel Antonio National Park which is the most visited park in the country, or a catamaran trip to Tortuga Island, are both highly recommended ways to spend the day.

There are many things to do in Jaco during your vacation, during the day and at night. Plan for both, but there are too many to list and describe them all on this page...
