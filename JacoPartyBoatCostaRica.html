The Jaco VIP Half Day Shoreline Cruise.

Pick up from your vacation rental villa in Jaco Beach is approximately 45 minutes prior to charter start time. We can make a quick stop to purchase some supplies if needed, but best to be prepared before hand. Also a good idea to eat something before leaving the house... or get the upgrade!

Once aboard your party boat, The Captain will take you north up the coast to where there are a couple of beaches nearly inacessible by land. Here you can party, jump in the ocean, do some snorkeling and swim up to the beach. After this, travel south to see Jaco Beach from the ocean.

Full Day Private Charter to Tortuga Island.

The most popular reason for the full day charter is to have a private boat take you to Tortuga Island. This nature reserve island is known for it's white sandy beaches, clear warm waters, ocean actvities and laid back vibe. Most of the ticket boats depart the island at around 2:30pm which may give you the opportunity to enjoy the beach and facilities for yourselves.

All party boat rentals include a Captain and at least one mate. They will do everything possible to keep you safe and to show you a good time. Rememeber to tip them at the end of the charter.

Important Notes and Details.

A non-refundable security deposit is required to book a party boat. That deposit will go towards the cost of the charter. If you cancel for any reason, you will lose the deposit. If the captain or boat owner cancels for any reason, you will be given a full refund.

Occasionaly, a captain or boat owner will have to cancel a charter for mechanical issues or bad weather. The captain and boat owner will always make safety the first priority. Jaco VIP does not own any of the party boats and has no control over cancellations.

Prices and details are subject to change. We do our best to keep this page and information up to date, but please contact us for the most recent updates or changes if any.
