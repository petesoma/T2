50' Private Party Boat Rental in Costa Rica

This is without a doubt THE BEST EXCURSION in Jaco for a group of friends and family on vacation in Costa Rica. Whether in Jaco for the nature or the nightlife imagine cruising the pacific ocean with some food, drinks and a DJ spinning your favorite tunes. Jump off the deck into the warm ocean water where you can do some snorkeling or simply relax on an inflattable donut.

Choose between a half day shoreline cruise around some private beaches near Jaco and Los Sueños (approximately 4 hours), or chartering a full day trip which includes going to Tortuga Island for lunch, snorkeling and a beautiful white sand beach. The Jaco VIP 50' Party Boat Half Day Shoreline Cruise.

A private bus will come pick you up from your vacation rental house at approximately 11:15am. The driver can make a quick stop to purchase some supplies if needed, but best to be prepared before hand. Also a good idea to eat something before leaving the house... or get the sandwich upgrade!

The Party Boat is anchored in Herradura Bay next to Los Sueños Marina which is approximately a 15 minute drive from Jaco. Once aboard, your captain will cruise around the area to some secluded beaches which are a few minutes to the north. Here you can party, chill out, jump into the water, and swim up to the beach. After this you will take a quick trip south to see the Jaco Beach skyline from the water.
