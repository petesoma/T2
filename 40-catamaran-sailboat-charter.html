The Only Private Sail Boat Available in Jaco Beach! ⛵

Half Day Shoreline Cruise to Secluded Beach.

Transportation to the party boat is included within Jaco (1 pick up and drop off location only). Pick up is at approximately 11:15am. The driver can make 1 quick stop at a grocery store if needed, but it is highly recommended to be prepared before hand so as not to waste time. It is a good idea to eat something before leaving the house... or get the sandwich upgrade (see below)!

The 40 foot luxury catamaran party boat is anchored in Herradura Bay next to Los Sueños Marina which is approximately a 15 minute drive from Jaco. Once aboard, your captain will cruise around the area to some secluded beaches a few minutes to the north. Here you can party, chill out, jump into the water, and swim up to the beach. After this take a quick trip south to see Jaco Beach from the water.

Most of the ticket boats depart the island at around 2:30pm which may give you the opportunity to enjoy the beach and facilities for a couple of hours to yourselves.
